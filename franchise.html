Frequently Asked Questions

What is the initial investment for a Wax-Wox franchise?

The investment varies based on location and scale, but it is structured to be significantly lower than traditional car wash models, focusing on van setup, equipment, and licensing fees. Specific details are provided in our Franchise Partner Guide.

Do I need prior experience in car washing?

No! We provide comprehensive training covering all aspects of the business, including our unique cleaning technology, operational management, and marketing strategies.

How does the Wax-Wox app benefit me?

The app acts as your primary source of sales. It manages booking, payment, routing, and customer communication, allowing you to focus purely on service delivery.

What kind of support does the franchisor offer?

We offer ongoing support, including marketing materials, proprietary cleaning product supply, technical support for the app, and regular business coaching.
