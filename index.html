Get to know WaxWox

WaxWox is revolutionizing car cleaning since 2018 with a clear focus on environmental friendliness and efficiency for corporate fleets. Our method, which saves over 99% of water compared to traditional vehicle cleaning, is revolutionary and helps fleet operators dramatically reduce the Company's environmental footprint.

The WaxWox mobile app makes fleet management easier by allowing vehicle cleaning, care and preparation to be booked anywhere and at any time, without the vehicles having to be taken to an external service provider. This mobile service not only saves valuable time, but also reduces CO2 emissions as fewer trips are required for vehicle care and preparation.WaxWox has already presented an impressive track record: thousands of vehicles are cleaned every year, which corresponds to significant savings in drinking water and a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions. Every cleaned vehicle contributes to achieving the goals of the European Green Deal and companies can clearly communicate their contribution to environmental protection.

With the increase in environmental regulations and demand for green services, WaxWox is positioning itself as a leader in environmentally friendly vehicle care for corporate fleets. We offer not just a cleaning service, but a comprehensive, sustainable and digital care solution designed specifically for the needs of modern fleets.With our unique technology and focus on sustainability, our goal is to actively shape the future of vehicle care.
